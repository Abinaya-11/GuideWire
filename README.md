# 🛡️ RiderShield: The Weather-Resilient Safety Net
> *Protecting the backbone of India's gig economy, one delivery at a time.*

## 📌 The Problem: "Rainy Days Mean Empty Pockets"
In cities like Chennai, heavy monsoon rains or extreme heat-waves don't just slow down delivery drivers (Zomato, Swiggy, Zepto)—they stop their income entirely. Currently, if a rider can't work due to weather conditions, they simply lose their daily wages, with no insurance or safety net to catch them.

## 💡 Our Solution: The Automated "Micro-Payout"
RiderShield is a **Parametric Insurance Platform** for delivery riders. 
- **The Micro-Fee:** Riders pay a small, sustainable weekly fee (e.g., ₹50/week).
- **The Trigger:** Our system monitors real-time weather data autonomously.
- **The Automatic Payout:** If rainfall exceeds **15mm/hour** or temperatures pass **45°C**, the system triggers a payout (e.g., ₹300) directly to the rider. 
- **Zero Paperwork:** 100% automated. If the API confirms the event, the payout follows.
